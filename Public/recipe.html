Mango Float

Mango float is a no bake Filipino desert layedred with graham crackers, sweetned whipped cream, and ripe mangoes. It needs to be chilled for atleast 3-4 hours

Ingredients:

  • Mangoes: 3-5 ripe yellow
  • Cream: 2 cups all-purpose or heavywhipping cream, chilled
  • Milk: 1 can sweetened condensed milk, chilled
  • Crackers: 1-2 packs graham crackers

Instruction:

  1. Prepare the cream: Whip chilled cream until stiff peaks form, then fold in condensed milk.
  2. Layering: Spread a thin layer of cream mixture in the bottom of adish.
  3. Assemble: Add a layer f graham crackers.
  4. Add filli: spread cream, over crackers, followed by sliced mangoes.
  5. Repeat: REpeat cracker, cream, and mango layers untilingredients are used, ending with cream and mangoes.
  6. Garnish and chilled: Garnish with crushed graham crackers if desired. cover and freeze for 4-6 hours or overnight.

Tips for success:

  • Chill Ingredients: Use cold ungredients and a cold dish for faster whipping and a firmer set.
  • Mango Selection: choose ripe, sweet, yet firm mangoes.
  • Serving: Let it thaw slightly before slicing
